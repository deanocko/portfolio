Pick and Place Robots

Industrial Robotics
Project Overview
In this assessment we were tasked with using a Universal Robots UR5 on a linear rail and a UR3 to autonomously construct a brick wall. We had to design a safe work environment that allowed employees to work safely within proximity of the robots.

My implementation takes advantage of the reach of the UR5 with the linear rail by automatically determining the bricks out of reach of the UR3 and assigning them to the UR5. The fire extinguishers and smoke detectors are nearby to mitigate electrical fires and the fences prevent employees for putting themselves in harms way whilst allowing for one end for bricks to be loaded onto the platform and another for the constructed walls to be removed.

Due to bonus marks I achieved more than 100% for this assignment along with only two other students in the subject.
