Premier League Prediction

A DIY solution to predict the results of the English Premier League

Project Overview

I created this project as my friends and I wanted a different solution to allow us all to bet on the season long Premier League outcomes every week.

Play with an interactive example of this project!

Each friend had to enter in their predictions for each match by using W, L or D to represent Win, Loss or Draw respectively. The sheet would then automatically tally all points upon release of results and break them down by week and in total. I used conditional formatting with colour coding to easily show those performing well and those that needed to up their game!
