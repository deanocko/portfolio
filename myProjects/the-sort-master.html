The Sort Master

Industrial Robotics

Project Overview

Our team's invention, the SortMaster uses the Dobot Magician inside a space confined by light curtains to ensure safe operation. The Sort Master can be controlled by a user using GUI or pre-programmed to complete tasks autonomously.

After building out our simulation, we implemented pick and place functionality on the real Dobot Magician within a lab to show proof of concept. The joint and reach limits of the robot made the potential use cases interesting and considering this we were pleased with the versatility of our final design.
